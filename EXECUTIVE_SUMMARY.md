## CONTINGENCY RESPONSES

### If Panel Seems Bored/Disengaged
- **Action:** Share a surprising statistic (32% night, 63% preventable)
- **Tone:** Inject urgency and human impact
- **Redirect:** Ask a rhetorical question to reengage

### If Panel Asks Technical Deep-Dive (LSTM, hyperparameters, etc.)
- **Action:** Acknowledge, then redirect
- **Response:** "Those are implementation details. For this discussion, the key is: Does it work? Yes. Can we deploy? Yes."
- **Keep Focus:** Business value, not technical complexity

### If Panel Challenges ROI Numbers
- **Action:** Break down calculation transparently
- **Detail:** How the ₹5-10 crore savings is calculated (cost per accident + prevention rate estimate)
- **Offer:** "We're planning a rigorous pilot to validate these numbers"

### If Demo/Tech Fails
- **Backup Plan:** Show screenshots instead
- **Statement:** "The live demo isn't working, but you can see here how the interface looks"
- **Move On:** Don't get stuck, maintain momentum

### If Time Gets Short
- **Priority Cuts:** Skip detailed roadmap, go straight to call to action
- **Key Preserve:** Keep business impact metrics and ROI message
- **Tactic:** Say "I see we're running short—let me jump to why this matters"

### If Panel Asks About Competitors/Alternatives
- **Response:** "We're aware of other safety systems. What makes ours unique is the integration: AI + ML + domain expertise. Most platforms do one thing; we do all three."
- **Differentiator:** 124 years of Indian railway-specific data (not generic)
